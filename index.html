Welcome to my Page!

Narges is a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at Imperial College Business School, in the Analytics and Operations department. She is under the esteemed supervision of Dr. Reza Skandari in health operations and Prof. Marisa Miraldo in health economics. Affiliated with the Centre for Health Economics and Policy Innovation (CHEPI), Narges brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to her field.

Narges holds a Bachelor's and a Master's degree in Industrial Engineering-Optimization Systems from Sharif University of Technology in Iran. Her academic journey and professional experiences have shaped her profound expertise in several key areas:

  • Health Operations Management and Analytics
  • Clinical Decision Making
  • Dynamic Programming

Narges's research is characterized by its interdisciplinary nature, bridging the gap between health economics and healthcare operations. Through her collaborations with esteemed doctors in the UK and the US, Narges aims to connect theoretical research with practical applications in healthcare, contributing to more effective and efficient practices.

Narges is preparing to enter the job market in 2024/2025.
