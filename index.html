Welcome to my Page!

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at Imperial College Business School, in the Analytics and Operations department. I am working under the esteemed supervision of Dr. Reza Skandari in health operations and Prof. Marisa Miraldo in health economics. Affiliated with the Centre for Health Economics and Policy Innovation (CHEPI), I bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to my field.

I hold a Bachelor's and a Master's degree in Industrial Engineering-Optimization Systems from Sharif University of Technology in Iran. My academic journey and professional experiences have shaped my profound expertise in several key areas:

  • Health Operations Management and Analytics
  • Clinical Decision Making
  • Dynamic Programming

My research is characterized by its interdisciplinary nature, bridging the gap between health economics and healthcare operations. Through my collaborations with esteemed doctors in the UK and the US, I aim to connect theoretical research with practical applications in healthcare, contributing to more effective and efficient practices.

I am preparing to enter the job market in 2024/2025.
